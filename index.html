Tractor and Post Driver

General Fencing Ltd has a fully-equipped tractor and post rammer setup, perfect for rural fencing jobs. Our custom engineered fencing trailers are setup with all the equipment to get your job done efficiently and to a high standard. Tough and rocky ground is no problem with our custom made fencing spike and rock drill.

Our side mounted post driver has multi-direction hydraulic controls and extension capabilities. No matter the terrain, we can get the job done!

Waikato based

General Fencing Ltd is based in Pirongia and operates in the greater Waikato region.

Rural fencing is our specialty

General Fencing Ltd specialises in farm and lifestyle block fencing. Whether it's building a new electric fence for your dairy farm, a post and rail fence to keep the dog in the yard or an 8 wire batten sheep fence, we have the tools and experience to do it all.

Fence maintenance & Urgent Repairs

Sometimes, a whole new fence isn't what you need. If you have a sturdy fenceline that just needs some TLC, we've got you covered too. We can straighten up and re-batten, replace broken posts, repair gateways and more.

We can also assist with urgent fence repairs, such as accidental damage from vehicles or stock. Call us today to see what we can do for you.
