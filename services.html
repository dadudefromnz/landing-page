Batten Fencing

General Fencing Ltd has the experience and equipment to build you a top notch 7 or 8 wire batten fence. We can even refurbish your existing one.

These are perfect for sheep fencing, and outriggers can be installed for keeping larger animals from interfering with the battens over time.

Post & Rail

Post and rail fencing is a sturdy and good looking fence for your farm or lifestyle block. These are a great option for driveways, boundaries or roadsides.

We can also build custom solutions for your job, such as tree boxes, stiles, and cattle yards.

We understand not every job is the same. The width between rails, number of rails and height from the ground can also be customised for your individual requirements. Whatever you want to keep in (or out) we have a fencing solution for you.
